Riverathon

Revive. Restore. Rejuvenate.

Revive, Restore & Rejuvenate river using LiDaR data. This hackathon brought to you by Amity School of Engineering & Technology in collaboration with National Mission for Clean Ganga aims to bring together innovative minds to develop solutions integrating emerging technologies with LiDaR technology for conservation, preservation & rejuvenation of our rivers.

What is Riverathon?

Join forces with fellow river enthusiasts – students, researchers, and techies – to tackle critical river challenges. Using cutting-edge LiDAR technology, we'll map, analyze, and design solutions for a healthier, more resilient river ecosystem. From flood mitigation to biodiversity mapping, your innovative ideas can contribute to sustainable river restoration.

Unleash your creativity and compete for exciting prizes! The Riverathon provides a platform to collaborate, learn, and make a real difference in river conservation. Let's harness the power of technology and human ingenuity to revive our rivers for generations to come.
